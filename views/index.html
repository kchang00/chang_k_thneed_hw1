What Is the Thneed?

The Thneed is a highly versatile object knitted from the foliage of a Truffula Tree. In the words of its founder, the Thneed is "a revolutionary product that will change the world as we know it!"

Why Do You Thneed It?

It has a million uses! Use it as a trendy hat, a tight-rope for an acrobat, a net for catching butterflies, or for exercise. It can also be used as a cleaner, or a swimsuit. The possibilities are endless.
